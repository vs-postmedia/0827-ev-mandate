New EV rules not popular in B.C.

Starting next year, 26 per cent of new cars sold in B.C. must be electric vehicles. Over half of British Columbians oppose the the rule, according to a new poll.

NOTE: The sample consisted of of 801 British Columbians who were surveyed online. Results are accurate to within +/- four percentage points, 19 times out of 20.

Source: Ipsos for Energy Futures
